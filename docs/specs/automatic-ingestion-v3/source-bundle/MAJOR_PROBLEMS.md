# Major Problems — Resolution Index

The major design issues are no longer left as unowned caveats. Their selected controls and residual risks are recorded in [`12-risk-resolution-register.md`](12-risk-resolution-register.md), and their implementation work is represented in [`plan/`](plan/).

The two additional v3 problems are:

1. **The question graph becoming an accidental knowledge ontology.** Resolved through distinct source, claim, question, answer, and document layers.
2. **One Questioner role compromising strict criticism with expansive curiosity.** Resolved through separate Evidence Critic and Curiosity Planner agents, schemas, contexts, workflows, and fixtures.

A problem is reopened only by a failed acceptance test, incident, changed policy, new format/provider constraint, or pilot evidence that invalidates the selected design.
