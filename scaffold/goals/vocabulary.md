# Goal vocabulary

Every question declares at least one goal. Free-form goals are allowed, but reuse these terms when they fit so evaluations remain consistent.

## implementation

The answer gives enough precise behavior, constraints, and edge cases to build and verify the feature.

## api-integration

The answer gives the external contract, authentication, requests, responses, errors, limits, and a usable integration example.

## security-review

The answer identifies assets, trust boundaries, attacker capabilities, relevant threats, mitigations, and unresolved assumptions.

## complexity-analysis

The answer states the applicable input dimensions and justifies time, space, network, or operational complexity.

## documentation

The answer explains the subject accurately at the depth requested and links to prerequisite or follow-on material.

## onboarding

The answer lets a new contributor reach a verified first success and explains the next useful steps.
