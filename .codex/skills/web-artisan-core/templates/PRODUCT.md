# PRODUCT

## Subject

What concretely exists in this product/site? Name the domain, objects, people, and real-world context.

## Audience

Primary users, expertise, environment, devices, accessibility needs, and emotional stakes.

## Job

The single most important job of this surface. Secondary jobs are explicitly ranked.

## Desired action or outcome

What should a person understand, decide, create, compare, or complete?

## Real content and data

Representative copy, entities, ranges, volumes, assets, and adversarial examples.

## Functional scope

Required routes, flows, interactions, integrations, and states.

## Constraints

Repository/platform, brand, performance, security/privacy, compliance, browser/device, timeline, and dependency constraints.

## Success conditions

Observable user and product outcomes. Include failure behavior and non-goals.

## Vocabulary

Canonical user-facing and code-facing terms. Avoid synonyms that cause drift.
