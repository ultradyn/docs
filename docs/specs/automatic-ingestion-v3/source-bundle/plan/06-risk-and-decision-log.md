Decisions intentionally left evidence-dependent:

1. lexical engine and runtime projection database;
2. workflow engine versus custom state machine;
3. first B-tier document formats;
4. model/provider assignment per role and data profile;
5. claim granularity after pilot labeling;
6. vector-retrieval activation threshold;
7. duplicate candidate thresholds;
8. navigation tasks and quality thresholds per project;
9. replay capsule retention and legal policy;
10. auto-merge eligibility for incremental imports;
11. perspective-sweep activation;
12. one-file-per-claim versus partitioned storage at scale.

Each decision receives an ADR containing candidate ideas, decisive goals, hard constraints, pilot evidence, selected option, migration impact, and reversal plan.
